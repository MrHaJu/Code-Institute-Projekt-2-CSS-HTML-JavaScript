Instructions

Start Screen:
When you launch the Quiz Game, you'll see the Start Screen.
You will be prompted to enter your Player Name.
After entering the name, click on the "Start Game" button to begin the quiz.

Quiz Gameplay:
Once the game starts, you will be presented with a series of 10 random questions one after the other.
Each question has four multiple choice answers, only one of which is correct.
Choose the answer you think is correct by clicking on the appropriate option.
After you select an answer, the correct answer will be displayed in green.
If you gave an incorrect answer, your answer will be shown in red and the correct answer will be shown in green.
If you click the "Next" button, the next question will be displayed.

Scoring:
At the end of the 10 questions, the Quiz Game will calculate your score.
The score is based on the number of correct answers you provided.
A correct answer earns you one point, and incorrect answers will not deduct points.
Your final score will be displayed on the screen.

Score Screen:
the Quiz Game will take you to the Score Screen to view your Score.
On the Score Screen, you will have two options:
"Play Again": Click on this button if you want to play the quiz again.
"End Game": Click on this button if you want to end the game.
It will close the Quiz Game and It will take you back to the Start Screen, where you can enter a new player name and start the quiz again or close the Tab.
Enjoy playing the Quiz Game and challenge yourself to get the highest score possible!
Test your knowledge on various topics and learn new things along the way.
